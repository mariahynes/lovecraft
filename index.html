Our Crafts

Love Crochet

Understand the basic stitches and work your way up to reading complex patterns. Our workshop videos will have you creating many items from tea-cosies to cushions and beyond!

Love Ceramics

Join us while we show you the techniques for creating ceramic art. Work along with us to create a memorable family heirloom.

Love Glass

Our glass fusion workshop will teach you the basics of glass slumping. Learn to create masterpieces for display on walls or tables.

Love Origami

This special craft serves many purposes - mindfullness included! Learn the building blocks for many origami shapes and turn your creations into decorations for all occassions.
